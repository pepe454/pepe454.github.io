  • Hello all, we are a group of high school seniors involved with the Massaco branch of the National Honor Society. We felt that many of the people who needed tutoring the most were not just located within our school but all across Connecticut, which is the purpose of this site. However, because location can be a limiting factor, we incorporated a videocall function into the site. In addition, because those who may need tutoring the most can not afford it, all tutoring sessions are completely free.
