  • We are a group of high school seniors from NHS who want to make it easier for students to find tutoring. This site allows students to schedule appointments with NHS tutors inside and outside of school. Even still many students are not able to make appointments outside of school due to logistical reasons. Because of that, our main goal is to provide a tutoring through video-calls using google hangouts.

Directions for videocall:

  1. Create a Google Hangout account if you do not already have one.
  2. Schedule an appointment. We will provide with you a tutor and the tutor's email.
  3. On the scheduled time, click the button and enter the email of the tutor and call them.
  4. If they do not pickup, try to contact them through their email.
