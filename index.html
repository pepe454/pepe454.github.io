  • We are a group of high school seniors who want to help tutor many students all across Connecticut. Some of these students are not able to make appointments outside of school due to logistical reasons. Because of that, our main goal is to provide a free live tutoring service for all students through video-calls and other features.

Directions for videocall:

  1. Create a Google Hangout account if you do not already have one.
  2. Enter the email of the tutor you are scheduled to meet with and call them.
  3. Be sure that the time is scheduled; if they do not pick up try their email.
